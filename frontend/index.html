What Is Save The Woods?

Save The Woods is a wildfire prevention and awareness tool. It uses input from satellite and crowdsourcing to determine if a wildfire has started, and alerts people near the fire. Additonally, the app (not currently released) awards points when you do activities such as read articles about wildfires, or go for a hike through the woods and check for wildfire risks.
